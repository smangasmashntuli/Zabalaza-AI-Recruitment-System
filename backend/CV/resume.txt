A resume is a professional document that summarizes your skills, experience, and education to help you secure job interviews.
Key Components of a Resume
A strong resume typically includes:

Contact Information: Full name, phone number, email, and optionally LinkedIn or portfolio links.
Professional Summary or Objective: A brief statement highlighting your career goals and key qualifications.
Work Experience: List relevant jobs with achievements, responsibilities, and measurable results.
Education: Degrees, certifications, and relevant coursework.
Skills: Technical, soft, and industry-specific skills.
Additional Sections: Awards, volunteer work, or projects that demonstrate your qualifications.
